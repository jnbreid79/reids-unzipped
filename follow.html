Follow Zipper

We're out there somewhere — if you want to you can keep up with where we are, what we're doing, and what we're seeing along the way.

Here's a guide to a few different ways you can follow along, and what each one is best for.

Real-time position
PredictWind Tracker

See exactly where Zipper is right now. The tracker shows our position updated every 15 mins when we're underway, along with how fast we're going, in which direction and the current wind conditions. We try and post short updates of life onboard each day when we're on passage, and photos of our anchorage when we arrive. You can trace the track back to see everywhere we've been.

Best for: Where we are · Where we've been
