Captain Underpants is our intrepid explorer and most successful blue cod fisherman. In his first year at Zipper-school he sailed through the 'sea turtles' class with flying colours, setting him up for a hugely successful year at Whangarei Primary where he excelled, made lots of friends, and became an expert defender in the hockey team.

Back onboard it's obvious how much Angus has grown up since our first year sailing — he's now a genuinely helpful crew member taking responsibility for himself and keeping us all entertained with an imaginative range of activities and games!
