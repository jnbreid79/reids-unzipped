Main systems
  • ⚙️ Engines Two 30 hp diesel engines, each with alternator and voltage regulator — the advantage of a catamaran, we have two of lots of things!
  • ⚡ Electrics Three 200 Ah lithium batteries, 3.5 kW inverter, and 1 kW of solar panels
  • 💧 Watermaker D60 Dessalator — produces approximately 50 L per hour
  • 🧭 Navigation Raymarine instruments, AIS and radar with Axiom chart plotter and 2 deck repeaters
  • 🍳 Cooking Gas: 2 hobs and 1 oven. Electric (when the sun is shining): Ninja 15-in-1 Foodie and induction hob
  • 🚿 Hot water Calorifier (heats when running engine) and electric immersion heater
  • 📡 Communications Iridium Go satellite communicator for offshore use; VHF; Starlink mini
  • 🔧 Steering Dual helm with Raymarine hydraulic autopilot
